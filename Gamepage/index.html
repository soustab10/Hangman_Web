How to Play
Welcome to Hangman.
Guess an unkown word in less than 6 tries, to put your name up on the Leaderboard.
NOTE:
Easy Level:
  • Word Length: 0-6
  • Time: 120 seconds
Medium Level:
  • Word Length: 7-9
  • Time: 90 seconds
Hard Level:
  • Word Length: 10-∞
  • Time: 60 seconds
Hearts: You have 6 hearts to get the word successfully.
HINTS: You will get only 3 Hint.
  • Hint 1: Reveals the defintion of the word.
  • Hint 2: Reveals missing letters of the word.
  • Hint 3: Reveal missing letters of the word.

Keyboard: You can either use your physical keyboard or the on-screen keyboard.
To kepp track of the letters that are not present in the word, refer the "Letters not present" section.
